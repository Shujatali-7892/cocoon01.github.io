Admissions At Cocoon Safe Heaven

Choosing your child’s school is an important milestone and we are happy to know that you are considering CSH as the school of choice for your child.
Team CSH is committed to help you make an informed decision and facilitate the admission process to be a transparent and stress-free experience.

Admission Process

Our aim is to keep our admission process smooth, transparent and stress free. Please take some time to go through and understand the steps to our admission process.
You will find the link to our online application form below. Please fill this form with careful deliberation as it will be the first step to starting the enrolment process for your child at CSH.

Step 1
Online Application

Your first step in applying for admission at CSH. Please take your time in carefully deliberating and filling in responses.

Step 2
Shortlisted applicants invited for info session

Shortlisted applicants are invited for an info session to gain a better understanding of the life and philosophy at CSH

Step 3
Interview and assessment

Shortlisted applicants are scheduled for an interview and assessment at CSH.

Step 4
Internaland review

Management reviews application, interview and assessment to form a decision.

Step 5
Decision sent via email

In case of acceptance, the email will guide parents towards the next steps for enrolment and fee payment.

Step 6
Enrolment and documents submission

To reserve your seat at CSH, an enrolment form must be filled along with submission of relevant documents and fee payment.
